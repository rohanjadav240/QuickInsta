The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has announced a key change for the upcoming season, shifting the IPL 2026 final from Bengaluru to Ahmedabad. The playoffs will now be held in Mullanpur and Dharamsala, as part of the revised schedule.

According to the official statement, the decision was taken due to “certain requirements from the local association and authorities that were beyond BCCI’s established guidelines and protocols,” leading to the venue reassignment.

The final will now take place at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, continuing its recent trend of hosting major cricketing events.
